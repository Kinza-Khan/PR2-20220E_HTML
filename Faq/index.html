Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We're here to answer!! If you don't see your question here drop us a line on our Contact Page

CAN I RETURN AN ITEM?

Yes! We accept returns for any reason within 30 days of your order. Just send us an email with your Order Number and we'll send you a return label.

WHEN WILL I RECIVE MY ITEM?

5 - 7 business days. We use UPS Priority shipping and pack your order by hand to make sure that everything is correct. If you need something faster, Let us know!
